Shattered Ascension

This is currently a stable map generator for the epic board game Twilight Imperium and/or Shattered Ascension. SA is an unofficial addition to the board game featuring additional rules and extended content. This is intended to be a high-quality archive of good and balanced present maps. Therefore, uploaded maps go through a moderation system.

Ultimately the goal of this website is to become a playable online version of TI / SA and also bring more fans into the genre. It is our hope that we can support Fantasy Flight Games for some additional purchases because a board game adaption will never be as good as the real immersion of the grand sci-fi game!
